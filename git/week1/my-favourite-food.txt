Momo (Nepalese dumplings) Recipe

Ingredients:

Flour, water (for dough)
Minced meat or vegetables
Onion, garlic, ginger, green chili
Soy sauce, salt, pepper, coriander

Instructions:

Make a dough with flour and water; let it rest.
Mix minced meat/veggies with onion, garlic, ginger, chili, soy sauce, salt, pepper, coriander.
Roll dough into circles, put filling inside, fold and seal.

Serve hot with dipping sauce.
