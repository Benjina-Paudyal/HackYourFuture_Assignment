Selroti (Nepali Sweet Rice Doughnut) Recipe

Ingredients:
2 cups rice flour
Â½ cup sugar
1 cup milk or water
1 tsp cardamom powder

Oil or ghee for frying
Instructions:
Mix rice flour, sugar, cardamom, and milk/water to make a smooth batter. Let it rest 30 min.
Heat oil/ghee in a deep pan.
Pour batter in a circular motion to form a ring shape.
Fry until golden brown on both sides.
Serve warm.
